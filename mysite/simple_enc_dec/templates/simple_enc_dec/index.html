Simple Atbash Encryption Cipher

  • The Web application provides a GUI for encrypting and decrypting Plain/Encrypted Text based on Atbash Cipher
  • Atbash Algorithm is a monoalphabetic cipher and mapping it to its reverse.
  • A is mapped to Z, B is mapped to Y and so on similarly in similar order.
  • Characters other than alphabets are unchanged.
